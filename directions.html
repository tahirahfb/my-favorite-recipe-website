Directions to Make Southern Style Grits


  1. Add water, heavy cream, and half n half to a pot
  2. Bring to a boil
  3. Slowly add in the grits will whisking steadily
  4. Continue to stir/whisk for 5 minutes
  5. Reduce heat to low and cover
  6. Cook for 7 to 10 minutes, whisk frequently
  7. Add butter, salt, pepper, and cheese
  8. Mix ingredients well
  9. Remove from heat

Serve Grits Immediately

For the best results, serve grits immediately, to avoid thickening.
